1. Common Equity Tier 1 capital Article 26 of CRR
Common Equity Tier 1 capital shall comprise capital instruments, share premium, retained earnings, and other reserves.

2. CET1 capital instruments Article 28 of CRR
Capital instruments shall be paid-up and not funded by the institution.

3. CET1 deductions Article 36 of CRR
Institutions shall deduct goodwill and intangible assets from CET1.

4. CET1 capital ratio Article 92(2)(a) of CRR
The CET1 capital ratio shall be at least 4.5% of total risk exposure.

5. CET1 ratio calculation
CET1 ratio = CET1 capital / Total risk exposure amount Ã— 100%

6. Tier 1 capital Article 25 of CRR
Tier 1 capital shall consist of the sum of Common Equity Tier 1 capital and Additional Tier 1 capital.

7. Own funds composition Article 72 of CRR
The own funds of an institution shall consist of the sum of its Tier 1 capital and Tier 2 capital.

8. Share premium accounts Article 26(1)(b) of CRR
Share premium accounts related to Common Equity Tier 1 instruments shall be included in CET1 capital.

9. Retained earnings Article 26(1)(c) of CRR
Retained earnings and other accumulated comprehensive income shall be included in CET1 capital.
