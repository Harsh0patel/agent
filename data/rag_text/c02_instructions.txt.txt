1. Total risk exposure Article 92(3) of CRR
Total risk exposure equals sum of credit risk, market risk, and operational risk weighted exposure amounts.

2. Total capital requirement Article 92(1) of CRR
Institutions must maintain total capital of at least 8% of total risk exposure amount.

3. Minimum capital ratios Article 92 of CRR
CET1 minimum 4.5%, Tier 1 minimum 6%, Total capital minimum 8%.

4. Capital calculation
Total capital requirement = Total risk exposure Ã— 8%

5. Credit risk exposure Article 92(3)(a) of CRR
Risk-weighted exposure amounts for credit risk shall be calculated using Standardised Approach or IRB Approach.

6. Capital adequacy
Total own funds must be sufficient to cover all risk exposures at the minimum required ratios.

7. Risk-weighted assets
Total risk exposure amount is the denominator for calculating capital ratios.

8. Own funds requirement Article 92(1)(c) of CRR
The total capital ratio expressed as own funds as a percentage of total risk exposure amount shall be at least 8%.

9. Risk exposure calculation Article 92(3) of CRR
Total risk exposure amount shall be calculated as the sum of risk-weighted exposure amounts for credit risk, plus own funds requirements for market risk and operational risk multiplied by 12.5.
