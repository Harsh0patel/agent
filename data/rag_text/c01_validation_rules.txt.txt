1. CET1 capital must be positive
Common Equity Tier 1 capital amount must be greater than zero.

2. CET1 ratio minimum threshold
CET1 capital ratio must be at least 4.5% of total risk exposure amount.

3. Tier 1 capital composition
Tier 1 capital must equal the sum of CET1 capital and AT1 capital.

4. Own funds calculation validation
Own funds must equal Tier 1 capital plus Tier 2 capital.

5. Deductions from CET1
Goodwill and intangible assets must be deducted from CET1 capital items before calculating final CET1 capital.

6. Share capital validation
Paid-up share capital must be fully paid and not funded by the institution itself.

7. CET1 components validation
CET1 capital must include share capital, share premium, retained earnings, and accumulated other comprehensive income.
