1. Total risk exposure must be positive
Total risk exposure amount must be greater than zero.

2. Total capital requirement minimum
Total capital requirement must be at least 8% of total risk exposure amount.

3. Capital adequacy check
Institution must maintain sufficient own funds to meet all minimum capital ratios.

4. Risk-weighted assets validation
Total risk exposure must equal sum of credit, market, and operational risk exposures.

5. Minimum total capital ratio
Total capital ratio must not fall below 8%.

6. Own funds requirement calculation
Own funds requirement equals total risk exposure amount multiplied by 8%.

7. Capital ratio hierarchy
If total capital ratio is below 8%, the institution fails to meet minimum regulatory requirements.
